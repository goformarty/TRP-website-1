OUR STUDIO

We are a team of professionals

The Regeneration Practice


is London-based architectural studio recognised extensively in awards, publications and exhibits in UK and internationally. Established in 1995 by Paul Latham, the firm enjoys successful history of creating innovative, environmentally responsible and thoughtful about its past architecture that stand the test of time.
TRP has a diverse workload spanning across the fields of education, housing, retail, industrial, office, theatre and historic building conservation and public realm improvements.




Our reputation has been built upon providing creative and intelligent architectural solutions that meet their operational brief without compromise. We seek to be proud of everything we do, from concept to delivery.

Each project is approached as a unique challenge. We start by listening to client needs and aspirations and conduct a logical analysis of the site. We aim to find the appropriate response that feels intuitively correct for each individual project or situation, based on its client, site, program and context — cultural, historical, economic, social and political.

We are passionate about using our design abilities and dedication to improve the built environment where we have the opportunity to do so.

And above all we enjoy the process of making good architecture.
