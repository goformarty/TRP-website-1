High House Farm

Previous Next


Details

Location:Grays, Essex

Category:Commercial

Client:Thurrock Council



Summary

High House Farm site consists of a Grade 2 Listed House, Farm Buildings and a Dovecote. Our approach was to absorb new uses including a cafe, multi-purpose events hall, meeting and teaching facilities, new office building and heritage display areas into the site with minimum impact on its character.

To retain the character of a Farm we restrained lighting levels by use of indirect lighting. The original barn winnowing doors were restored complete with giant handmade iron strap hinges. These doors can be opened dramatically for events creating “break out” space into the former Rick Yard to the south, itself presented as a wild flower meadow.

The barn was upgraded thermally and acoustically.
